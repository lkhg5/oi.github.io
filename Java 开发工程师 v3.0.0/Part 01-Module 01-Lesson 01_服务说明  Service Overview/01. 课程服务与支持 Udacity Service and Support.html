01. 课程服务与支持 Udacity Service and Support

欢迎加入新的纳米学位之旅！Welcome to Your Udacity Nanodegree Program!

相信优秀的你可以凭借优秀的自主学习和自我管理能力，以超高性价比获得同样的知识与技能。为提高学习效率，请仔细阅读以下内容，了解在本纳米学位中，你将获得的学习服务与支持。

For those with independent learning abilities and self-management skills, you will access world-class curriculum and learn from top industry professionals. To schedule your program reasonally, please read carefully and get well aware of all services and supports available during your term.

课程服务 Service

在课程有效期内，我们仍将提供专家项目审阅（英文）来帮助你来确认学习结果；本课程将以全英文的形式展开，通过所有必修实战项目后，你可以在教室中完成毕业流程并获得英文版证书。你可以进入 ND Program Home（返回上一层）查看学期结束时间。

During your term, our qualified English reviewers will provide detailed feedback on your submitted projects and help ensure you learn the skills the right way. The program is taught in English. You will need to complete all the lessons and projects in order to receive your graduation certificate in English. Go back to ND Program Home to view your term deadline.

学习支持 Support

当你遇到问题时，如果它是学习上的问题，我们推荐你充分使用 Google、 Stack Overflow 等搜索引擎或垂直社区；对于其他问题，如果是与账号、课程加入、发票相关的常见问题，你可以先查看我们的常见问题中心，或许可以更快获得答案，也可随时点击此处 提交工单,与我们服务支持团队取得联系。

If you have content questions, you may look for answers using web-searching methods like Google or Stack Overflow. You may also try searching in online learning communities. For general inquiries related to account, course access, invoice etc., click here to submit a Requset form and our support team will be in touch. You may also view FAQ for a quick solution.

期待你顺利毕业！ Looking forward to your graduation!
